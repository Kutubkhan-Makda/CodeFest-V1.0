About


Those who love and are familiar with South Indian food will know how diverse it is, and how the various regional dishes come packed with punch and flavor. A simple dish of sambar with rice can be wholesome and delicious, making you feel happy and satisfied to the core. While rice is an important ingredient in most south Indian dishes, there are multiple delicious options the cuisine offers, using dals too besides meat. There are plenty of options for days where you prefer something light, cool and homely, and also for days you are particularly craving something spicy and tantalising. The options are endless and drool-worthy to say the least.
